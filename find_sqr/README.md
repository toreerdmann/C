
# `find_sqr`

Find the biggest square that can fit between the obstacles in a rectangular
map.

## Examples

Below are some example outputs:

    ./bin/mkmap 5 5 10 | ./bin/find_sqr

    ..xxx
    .oxxx
    ..xxx
    .....
    .o.o.


    ./bin/mkmap 5 5 10 | ./bin/find_sqr

    xx...
    xx...
    .o..o
    o....
    .....


    ./bin/mkmap 10 10 10 | ./bin/find_sqr

    oxxxxxxxo.
